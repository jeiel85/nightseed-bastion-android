# 05_LEVEL_AND_CONTENT_DESIGN

## First Map: Moonwell Bastion

### Layout Goals

- Three readable enemy lanes.
- Central core visible from start.
- Build slots positioned around lane choke points.
- No offscreen combat during first vertical slice.
- Camera fixed or lightly following hero; no RTS camera complexity.

### Lane Roles

| Lane | Identity | Threat Pattern |
|---|---|---|
| North Ridge | Slow tank lane | Brutes and wall pressure |
| East Hollow | Swarm lane | Huskling groups and runners |
| South Gravepath | Ranged/elite lane | Hex archers and support units |

## Night Progression

| Night | Teaching Goal | Main Threat |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Basic building and combat | Huskling |
| 2 | Fast enemies | Bone Runner |
| 3 | Walls and traps | Grave Brute |
| 4 | Dusk Bargain | Mixed swarm |
| 5 | Support disruptor | Lantern Eater |
| 6 | Ranged pressure | Hex Archer |
| 7 | Boss integration | Nightseed Herald |

## Content Naming Rules

Use original Nightseed vocabulary.

Good:

- Moonwell
- Ember Brazier
- Grave Snare
- Lantern Eater
- Nightseed Herald
- Dusk Bargain

Avoid:

- Names strongly associated with existing games
- Generic copied building roles with identical naming clusters
- Exact fantasy faction names from recognizable IPs
