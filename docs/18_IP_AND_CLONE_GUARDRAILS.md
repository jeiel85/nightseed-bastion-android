# 18_IP_AND_CLONE_GUARDRAILS

## Core Rule

Nightseed Bastion may be inspired by broad genre patterns, but it must remain an original game in presentation, content, structure, and expression.

## Do Not Copy

- Existing title names or similar confusing names.
- Exact UI hierarchy or menu screen composition.
- Exact building sets and upgrade naming.
- Specific enemy silhouettes or faction identities.
- Specific map layouts.
- Marketing copy.
- Logos, fonts, or icon shapes recognizable from another game.
- Exact stat curves or economy pacing from another title.

## Safe Inspiration Areas

- General day/night structure.
- General tower defense principles.
- General action-strategy hybrid controls.
- General roguelite reward choices.
- Broad dark fantasy atmosphere.

## Required Originalization

Nightseed Bastion must emphasize:

- Nightseed lore and corruption mechanics.
- Dusk Omen and Dusk Bargain as named original systems.
- Portrait mobile-first UX.
- Pixel-art dark fantasy rather than low-poly miniature aesthetics.
- Run-based fortress evolution and meta progression.

## Asset Safety

Generated images and concept art are references unless manually cleaned and verified. Before release, all UI, logos, icons, and characters should be reviewed for originality and text correctness.
